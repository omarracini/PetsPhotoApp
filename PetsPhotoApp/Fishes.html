Types of fish

Freshwater fish:

  • Guppies.
  • Tetras.
  • Betta fish.
  • Discus fish.
  • Angel fish.

Saltwater fish:

  • Clownfish.
  • Gobies.
  • Damselfish.
  • Moray eels.
  • Butterflyfish.

Here are some tips for choosing aquarium fish:

  • Start with small, hardy fish.
  • Choose fish that are compatible with each other.
  • Research the specific needs of each species.
  • Buy fish from a reputable store.
